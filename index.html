📐 Lightbox Formula

Edit the area calculation formula for each lightbox style. Use variables: w (width), h (height), d (depth) - unit is cm. Result is in m².

📖 Other Formulas

Raised letters: Area = 0.9 × (Height in meters)² × Letter Count
Example: 10cm height letter = 0.9 × (0.1)² × 1 = 0.009 m²/letter
Logo/Panel: Area (m²) = Length (cm) × Width (cm) / 10000
Acrylic Logo: Round: (L × W + (2L + 2W) × 8) / 10000 | Square: L × W / 10000
